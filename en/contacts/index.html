MINISTRO PER L’INNOVAZIONE TECNOLOGICA E LA DIGITALIZZAZIONE

The Digital Transformation Team was born to build the “operating system” of the country, a series of fundamental components on top of which we ca build simpler and more efficient services for the citizens, the Public Administration and businesses, through innovative digital products. The team of the Commissioner has been created on September 16, 2016 and will last until September 16, 2018.
